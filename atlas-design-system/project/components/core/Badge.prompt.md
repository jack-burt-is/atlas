Small status or tier label in mono caps. Use for states ("IN PROGRESS"), counts, and achievement tiers.

```jsx
<Badge variant="gold" dot>In progress</Badge>
<Badge variant="success">Completed</Badge>
<Badge variant="platinum">Platinum</Badge>
```

Variants: `neutral`, `gold`, `success`, `info`, `danger`, the tiers `bronze | silver | platinum` (gold tier = `gold`), and `solid` (filled gold). `dot` adds a status dot; `icon` adds a leading node.

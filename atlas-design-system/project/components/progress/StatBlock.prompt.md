Big stat figure with a mono caption — used across dashboards for score, counts and coverage.

```jsx
<StatBlock label="Outdoor Score" value="14,820" gold size="lg" />
<StatBlock label="Munros" value="43 / 282" />
<StatBlock label="Day's reach" value="14" sub="peaks remaining" align="center" />
```

`gold` gives the gradient treatment reserved for hero numbers (score). Sizes `sm | md | lg`.
